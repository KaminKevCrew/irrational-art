Welcome to Irrational art!

You are at visiting the least rational site on the internet; The nav bar contains several buttons, and a text entry field. Each of the preset buttons will render 20 thousand digits of e, pi, phi, or root-two. If you choose to enter your own digits, those digits will be rendered! (decimal digits only please.)

Each individual digit corresponds to both a color, and a vector. The digit 0 will be a line segment straight up, and will have a gold-ish color. The line segments that correspond to each following digit continue around an imaginary circle like hands on a clock. However, we only have 10 directions instead of 12. There are white line segments that denote the beginning and end of each number.

Feel free to have a look around and give it a try yourself!
